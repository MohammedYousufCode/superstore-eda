## 🔍 Business Questions Answered
1. Which product category is most profitable?
2. Which region performs best in sales and profit?
3. What shipping mode do customers prefer?
4. Does discounting hurt profit?
5. Which sub-categories are loss-making?

---

## 💡 Key Findings
- Technology leads in profit margin (~17%). Furniture barely makes 2%.
- West region dominates both sales and profit.
- Discounts above 30% turn profitable orders into losses.
- Tables and Bookcases have negative total profit — pricing issue confirmed.
- Sales show strong year-end seasonality (November spike every year).
